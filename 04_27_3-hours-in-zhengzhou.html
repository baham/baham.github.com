About 3 Hours in Zhengzhou

Apr 27th, 2014

When I was in Pingdingshan, I need to came back to Xuzhou City to get my debit card.

So I asked 2 day for leave.

But the ticket of train from Pingdingshan to Xuzhou with a chair is gone. So I bought a ticket from Pingdingshan to Zhengzhou and a ticket from Zhengzhou to Xuzhou.

So I have about 3 hours to travel in Zhengzhou and to wait for the train.

Zhengzhou City is a big city in central China. It is the biggest city in Henan. Its name ccame from the Zheng Kingdom in Zhou Dynasty.

I did not really travel there, just bought something and wook the subway to the banks to ask about the visa debit card. Cause you know I need a visa card to pay the mensa test fee. That time, I think maybe there were some big backs would support some visa or master debit card. And I think the cards would get at once. In fact, my thought was wrong.

But it is not a bad experience. Cause I saw something.

I saw this poster on the wall near the road. And in winter holiday, I saw them in my hometown too.

There are 3 words there, maybe you can not understand them. Sincerely, I did not understand them too.

But the translations to English were:

  1. Thanks! My Country and parents!
  2. Sing a folk song for the P!
  3. Long Live the P!

I really can not understand these words. It is like in a old kingdom country not like in a modern country.

And this is show a phenomnon in China, Nearly everyone dislike the p, but most of people still want to be the member. maybe they want to get the power, the economic power, the trust by g.o.v and so on. So sometimes they lie.

And there another picture in tunnel of Zhengzhou subway station.

It means “Why China can be so strong? Cause we have the p.”

And I take a photo about this building.

It make me think of Tower Defense Games, like dota. LoL!!!
