Graduate

Jun 16th, 2014

Yesterday, brothers and sisters got together to learn God’s words and say goodbye to graduates, like me.

I came to the fellowship in September 2013, and now I will leave.

And I find a difference between I came and I left, before I came to the fellowship, I had a heart made up of stone, but now I feel my heart is made up of flesh. What or who gave me this difference, I thing is God’s words, it is Bible, it is Spirit.

I always thought I would not be sad when I graduate. But I find I am wrong, now. Because I feel sadness.

Before noon, Mrs Zhang call me to ask me about how to get water. And I just came to the fellowship. I arrived at about 12:30, and there are many elders and classmates, I felt sleepy, cause I had a habit to sleep at noon.

I did not know how to say. So I just write it sorted by the time.

I bought steamed breads and condiments.

We sang songs to admire and praise God.

Pastor Lu gave us a lesson about how to keep the faith after graduatation in this guilty World.

A presentation of graduates, and I am the first, I did not prepare fot it.

Receiving the gifts and elders gave us a blessing and lesson(Pastor Yao and Aunt Diao and so on).

Cells’ shows, and I paly a harmonica show and sang it /simple gift/.

We lined in 2 circles and said goodbye.

We ate. There are really many people this time. Maybe 40-50 people.

I really don't know how to describe this day, what I can do is just write the title and keep the memory in my mind.

Thanks God!

And he answering said, Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy strength, and with all thy mind; and thy neighbour as thyself. —Luke 10:27
