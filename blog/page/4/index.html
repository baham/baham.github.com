Take the Mensa Test

Apr 21st, 2014

About 2 years ago, I wanted to join mensa. Cause in iqtest.dk, I have gotten a 130 score.

But you know in 2012, the mensa.org.cn showed the number in 2012 is to achieve the max number. So I waited.

And the mensa.org.cn showed test will open in 2013.

But in 2013, the test did not open until the December in 2013.

And I knew there was something happen in mensa-china. But recently, the mensa-china.com opened, although I would be in internship in April 19, I still decided to ask for leave to take the test.

But I found this test must be paid by paypal, and the method of paying the fee doesn’t support alipay or unipay card.

So i asked Brother Jun to help me pay the fee by using his credit card(both visa and unipay). But I ignored one thing that paypal need use the credit card of the account register.

Then one bad thing happend. I did not pay the fee successfully, and the credit card of Brother Jun was freezed.

After that day, I called paypal to solve this.
