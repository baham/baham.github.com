I wanted to buy an elink ereader long long ago. But I did not buy it, cause I did not have enough money.

After I got my first week salary, I wanted to buy something for myself. I find my roomate Ning has a Kindle 3 ereader. I try to use it, it is pretty cool. And I find it has a enough good reading experienment, though it is an old version of Kindle. You know my salary is not enough to buy a Kindle Paperwhite. When I saw this ereaker, I decided not to buy kpw. After the contrasting with different ereaders, Kindle, kobo, nook. Finally, I decided to buy kobo glo.

  1. I need a erader with backlit;
  2. The price less than 650 yuan;
  3. Just an ereader, not an android device;
  4. Bigger screen resolution.

So what I contrast are kpw1, kpw2, nook3, nook4, kobo glo. The kobo glo is my last choice.

When you started to use it, you must need a wifi to connect kobo server to update or to buy books. And there are 2 interesting things, ‘Reading Stats and Rewards’, very cool tools.
